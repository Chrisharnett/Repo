Tips for Players

Tactics

In chess, a tactic refers to a sequence of moves that limits the opponent's options and may result in tangible gain.

Strategy

The art of chess strategy is knowing how to formulate a plan for the chess game, and arrange your chess pieces to accomplish this plan.
